Lunar Forge 1.0 is a 18-hour immersive hackathon organized by Coding Ninjas Saranathan Community, where builders, designers, and dreamers come together to forge the impossible. Push boundaries, break conventions, and create projects that redefine the digital landscape.

Whether you're a seasoned developer or a first-time hacker, Lunar Forge is your launchpad. Collaborate with brilliant minds, access cutting-edge tools, and compete for prizes that matter.
